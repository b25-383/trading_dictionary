RSI
Technical Analysis
A momentum oscillator measuring speed and magnitude of price changes. Readings above 70 suggest overbought, below 30 oversold conditions.
Pip
Forex
The smallest standardised move in a currency pair. In most pairs, 1 pip = 0.0001. It's the base unit for measuring gains and losses in Forex.
MACD
Technical Analysis
Moving Average Convergence Divergence. A trend-following momentum indicator showing the relationship between two EMAs.
Leverage
General
Borrowed capital used to increase potential return. A 10:1 leverage means you control $10,000 with $1,000. Amplifies both gains and losses.
