Market Trends

Identify bullish, bearish and consolidating / neutral market patterns.

⇑Bullish
Upward movement
Prices are consistently fluctuating in an upward direction. Buyers outweigh sellers creating higher highs and higher lows.
⇓Bearish
Downward movement
Prices are consistently fluctuating in a downward direction. Sellers outweigh buyers creating lower highs and lower lows.
⇔Consolidating
Sideways movement
Prices are fluctuating within a relatively narrow range, indicating a period of indecision between buyers and sellers.
