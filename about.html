Why the Trading Dictionary Exists

The one way to fight financial inequality is through education.

Nowadays jobs are competitive, and if not scarce they do not provide much due to increasing prices naturally as economies evolve.The rich are getting richer, and the poor are getting poorer, they only way to try eliminate this is by creating a platform that empowers traders with basic knowledge and resources to be able to financially empower themselves. With the help of Botswana Stock Exchange, we aim to bridge the gap between traditional financial education and modern trading practices.

The goal is to make financial literacy accessible — no jargon, no agenda, no costs, just an attempt to empower basic humans on how to choose and make informed investing.
